A Bit About Me

I'm a revenue-focused marketing professional with a background in SaaS, financial services, cybersecurity, and national media. I care about building things that work.

Currently, I lead demand generation and lifecycle marketing at American City Business Journals, supporting 25+ sales reps across 45 U.S. markets. I own the HubSpot infrastructure, run integrated campaigns, and spend a lot of time thinking about how marketing and sales can actually work together — not just in theory.

I tend to work best at the intersection of strategy and execution. I like figuring out what's broken, building systems to fix it, and measuring what happens next. I'm especially drawn to how AI and automation are reshaping what's possible for lean marketing teams.

Outside of work, I write poetry, record music, mentor college students, and am building a reading app in beta. Not all of it fits neatly on a resume — but I think it's part of the picture.
